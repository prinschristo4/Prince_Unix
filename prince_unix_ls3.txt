Filter commands.								Date:30-01-2018
1.head-prints the first nine lines of the list given 15.
2.tail-prints the last 10 lines of the list.(from 6th line).
3.cut-Remove or "cut out" sections of each line of a file or files
4.paste-pastes the line.
5.wc-counts the total no of lines, words and bytes.
6.sort-sorts the contents of a text file, line by line.
7.uniq-reports or filters out repeated lines in a file.
